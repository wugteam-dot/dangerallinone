The Author

Saša Slavić

Saša Slavić is a Croatian author of speculative and metaphysical fiction, exploring the hidden relationship between consciousness, reality, and origin.

His work focuses on the idea that existence itself may be structured, remembered, and influenced in ways humanity does not yet fully understand. Blending psychological depth with cosmic scope, Saša creates stories where identity is fluid, memory is infrastructure, and love becomes a force capable of stabilizing reality.

"Influenced by film, philosophy, and modern myth, Saša writes stories that unfold gradually, inviting readers to question perception, authorship, and the nature of creation itself."
